Point of Sale Email Receipt Odoo Apps

Send POS Email Receipt Odoo Apps

Enable or Disable POS Receipt Email Odoo Apps

POS Email Receipt Odoo App allows users to send electronic receipts to their customers. This app not only saves on printing costs but also contributes to environmental protection by reducing paper usage.

With the POS Receipt Email App, users have the option to enable or disable the option to send email receipts directly from the Point of Sales - POS configuration settings. This feature simplifies the process, allowing businesses to customize their receipt delivery method according to their preferences and customer needs.

The app offers the valuable functionality of sending receipts to additional email addresses associated with the customer. Also, customers can conveniently receive their receipts at multiple email addresses of their choice, ensuring that important purchase information is easily accessible to them.
